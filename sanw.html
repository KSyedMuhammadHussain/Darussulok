Darussulok

MAHANA ISLAAHI MAJLIS BANGALORE

A "Darusuluk Islahi wa Tarbiyat Majlis" is an Islamic gathering focused on spiritual reform (Islah) and upbringing/training (Tarbiyat). These gatherings are often held in homes, masjids, or institutions dedicated to nurturing spiritual growth and moral values.

Darusuluk provides an environment for learning, reflection, Zikr, and connecting with righteous scholars for guidance and spiritual elevation.

SILSILAH AALIYA NAQSHBANDIYAH KE WAZAAIF

1. ISTIGHFAR: Astagfirullaha Rabbee Minn-Kulli Zambeu-Wa Atoobi Elayh.

Subah - 100 Times & Shaam - 100 Times

2. DAROOD SHAREEF: Allahumma Salli Alaa Sayyidinaa Muhammad Wa Alaa Aali Sayyidinaa Muhammad Wa Barik Wa Sallim.

Subah - 100 Times & Shaam - 100 Times

3. TILAWAT-E-QURAN: Tilawat-e-Quran ki niyat se Quran ki tilawat karna.

Rozaana 1 Juz (30 Para) yaani 20 Pages ki tilawat karna.

4. MURAQABAH: Thodi Der Ke Liye Sub Kamon Se Hut Kut Kar Allah Ki Yaad Mein Gardan Jhuka Kar Baithna.

Yeh Niyyath Karna Kay Allah Ki Rahmath Aarahi Hai Mere Dil Mein Sama Rahi Hai...

5. WUQOOF-E-QALBI: Dil Ko Allah Ki Yaad Mein Gharq Karna.

Chalte, Phirte, Baithte, Uthte, Har Dum Tawajjo Allah Ki Taraf Rakhna.

6. RABTA-E-SHAYKH: Deen Seekhne Ke Liye Shaykh Apne Shaykh Se Rabta Rakhna.
