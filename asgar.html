A "Darusuluk Islahi wa Tarbiyat Majlis" is an Islamic gathering focused on spiritual reform (Islah) and upbringing/training (Tarbiyat). Darusuluk provides an environment for learning, reflection, Zikr, and connecting with righteous scholars for guidance and spiritual elevation.

بسم الله والحمد لله والصلاة والسلام على رسول الله وعلى أزواجه وآله وأصحابه

أَلاَ بِذِكْرِ اللّهِ تَطْمَئِنُّ الْقُلُوبُ

Translation: Surely in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find comfort. [الرعد: آیت 28]

True Hadith: The slave who remembers his Lord is like a living creature, and the one who does not remember Him is like the dead one. [بخاری: 6407 ؛ مسلم: 1823]

"...وَاذْكُر رَّبَّكَ كَثِيرًا وَسَبِّحْ بِالْعَشِيِّ وَالإِبْكَارِ"

Translation: And remember your Lord much and glorify Him in the evening and the morning. [آل عمران: آیت 41]

Sunnat Azkaar of the Morning and Evening

The numbers of all Ahadith are in accordance with the international numbering of the scholars of Haramain and Beirut.

  1. He who recites Ayat-al-Kursi (Al-Baqara: 255) becomes safe from Shaitan and Jinn.

    (1 time, in the morning and in the evening)

  2. Recitation of Muawwidhat will suffice you against everything.

    (3 times each in the morning and evening)

    قُلْ هُوَ اللَّهُ أَحَدٌ
    قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ الْفَلَقِ
    قُلْ أَعُوذُ بِرَبِّ النَّاسِ
  3. Whoever recites:
    لا إله إلا الله وحده لا شريك له، له الملك وله الحمد وهو على كل شيء قدير

    Gets the reward of freeing 4 slaves and protection for the day.

  4. Whoever says:
    رَضِيتُ بِاللهِ رَبًّا وَبِالإِسْلاَمِ دِينًا وَبِمُحَمَّدٍ نَبِيًّا

    Will be granted Jannah. (3 times, morning and evening)
